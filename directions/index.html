Directions

The conference will be held at the Sheraton Sheraton Centre Toronto Hotel.

There are two airports in Toronto: Toronto Pearson Airport and Billy Bishop Airport.

From Pearson, if you would like to take public transit you should first take the UP Express to Union Station. (3 stops, end of the line). Once at Union, transfer to Line 1 towards Vaughn, disembark at Osgoode (2 stops), and then walk east down Queen St W for a block. See here for the walk directions from Union station, or some TTC directions.

From Billy Bishop, once you leave the airport you can either (1) take a bus directly to Union Station that leaves roughly once every 15 minutes or (2) walk around 10 minutes north to the intersection of Queens Quay West at Bathurst St East Side and take the 509 Harbourfront Streetcar eastbound to Union Station. Once at Union, you transfer to Line 1 towards Vaughn, disembark at Osgoode (2 stops), and then walk east down Queen St W for a block. Rideshare services and taxis are also an option, especially for late/early arrivals when the trains are not running.
