Posters

The most common/standard poster size for CASCA-TO posters is A1 (vertical) or 594 x 841 mm or 23.4 x 33.1 inches. The maximum allowed size is 33 x 33 inches.

Some printing options include:

  • Staples: They are fast, you can upload the poster online and pick-up the next day. The closest one is at 375 University Avenue, https://shop.staplescopyandprint.ca/pages/signs-posters-banners
  • Print City: Address: 180 Bloor St W #105, Toronto, ON M5S 2V6 http://www.printcity.ca/
